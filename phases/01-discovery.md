# Phase 1: Discovery

**Objective:** Understand the problem space, explore the landscape, identify stakeholders, and establish constraints and success metrics.

---

## What is Discovery?

Discovery is about **exploration and understanding**. It's asking the right questions before committing to solutions. This phase prevents building the wrong thing.

**Output:** A clear problem statement, stakeholder analysis, constraints, and success metrics that the entire team agrees on.

---

## Key Activities

### 1. Define the Problem
- What problem are we solving?
- Why does it matter?
- Who is affected?
- What happens if we don't solve it?

### 2. Identify Stakeholders
- Who needs this?
- Who influences the decision?
- Who will use it?
- Who pays for it?
- What are their needs?

### 3. Explore the Current State
- What exists today?
- What's working?
- What's broken?
- Why isn't it solving the problem?

### 4. Document Constraints
- Time constraints
- Budget constraints
- Technical constraints
- Regulatory/compliance constraints
- Team skill constraints

### 5. Define Success
- How will we measure success?
- What does "done" look like?
- What are the non-negotiables?
- What's nice-to-have?

### 6. Risk Identification
- What could go wrong?
- What assumptions are we making?
- What dependencies exist?
- What unknowns do we have?

## Success Criteria for Discovery

Before moving to Requirements, validate:

- ✅ Problem statement is clear and concise
- ✅ All stakeholders have been identified
- ✅ Stakeholder needs are documented
- ✅ Current state is understood
- ✅ Constraints are explicit
- ✅ Success metrics are measurable
- ✅ Assumptions are documented
- ✅ Key risks are identified
- ✅ Team alignment is confirmed

---

## Common Discovery Mistakes

❌ **Jumping to solutions** - Focus on understanding the problem first
❌ **Missing stakeholders** - Talk to everyone, not just your boss
❌ **Vague success metrics** - "Better" isn't measurable
❌ **Ignoring constraints** - Reality will bite you later
❌ **Skipping this phase** - Technical debt starts here
